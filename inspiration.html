Inspiration for my Model
For my object I decided to create a building, when I went to Greece in July 2018 I took inspiration from the Acropolis of Athens, this is where one of the most famous buildings in the world is, the Pantheon. The Pantheon was a temple built for the goddess Athena. This is where I got the idea for the wrap around stairs and the roof of the building as they fit in with my original concept of a Greek-Italian/Roman Temple.

While on the same holiday we also went to Venice in Italy where I also drew inspiration for my building. We visited San Marco Square which is home to the Church of St. Mark. Here was where I got the idea for the pillars of the building as pillars here looked similar to the ones on my design.
